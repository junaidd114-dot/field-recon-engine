- **Broker Application** - the broker's original submission, including data from a previous approved application for the same customer
- **HP Agreement** - the hire purchase agreement generated by the finance company
- **Purchase Invoice** - the dealer's invoice for the vehicle
- **Supplier Declaration** - the dealer's signed declaration
- **Payment Mandate** - the customer's direct debit authorisation
- **Giro Slip** - the dealer's bank payment slip
- **Funds Form** - electronic payment instruction from the dealer
- **FCA Register Lookup** - FCA register lookup result for the dealer
- **System Check Results** - Gold Check and HPI vehicle finance check results
